Why a waitlist?

Our tickets last year sold out almost instantly and while we vastly increased the size of the venue to accomodate, we've also booked an even bigger lineup so we expect to sell out again.

After taking your feedback on board we have decided this year to have people show their interest by submitting their details to a waitlist - we'll be in touch next month to tell you whether you were successful or not, and if you are we will provide you with a link to purchase the tickets. Any tickets that are allocated but not purchased will go on general sale - the date of which will be announced at a later date.
